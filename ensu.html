Overview

Navigating your mental health is difficult. There are lots of apps that can help you keep track of your mood or bring you back to the ‘present’ moment but none that helps you check in on your peers and see how a tool such as music can help ‘uplift’ or ‘unwind’ your moods.

Technologies

As a small team, we took a hybrid mobile application development process with React Native being the main technology to build the front-end interface.

For testing and production development I used the Expo module + native Simulator to test on an array of iOS and Android devices.

To support the back-end, we used a combination of GraphQL queries and mutations via Apollo, written in Django (Python) on the backend and data supported by PostgresSQL.
